Overview

With the use of NFL play-by-play data from 2013-2019, we want to predict the play type called when teams are faced with 4th down situations. We plan to look at the following data: Quarter, Time Remaining, Down, Yards to Go, and Yardline. Additionally, we want to know how often plays are successful in converting for a first down or within range to kick a field goal.
